Queen of TikTok

One crown. 32 queens. Last one standing wins.

📜 How It Works

Tournament starts Thursday at 10:30 PM EST

1

32 queens → 8 groups of 4

Everyone who signs up with their TikTok username is randomly drawn into 8 groups of 4. This is the first elimination series.

2

Round 1 — Two strikes you're out

In each group of 4, the lowest score each round gets a strike. Two strikes and you leave. Play continues until one queen is left standing per group. The 8 winners advance.

3

Round 2 — 2 groups of 4, one strike

The 8 winners split into 2 groups of 4. Lowest score each round gets a strike, and one strike eliminates you. Last queen standing in each group makes the finals.

4

Finals — Best of 3

The two finalists go head-to-head. First to win 2 out of 3 is crowned Queen of TikTok 👑.

📜 The Rules

1

Sign-ups → 8 groups of 4 (32 queens)

Everyone who enters their TikTok username is randomly drawn into 8 groups of 4. This is the first elimination series.

2

Round 1 — Two-strike elimination

Within each group of 4, the lowest score each round receives a strike. Two strikes and you're out. Play continues until one queen is left standing in the group. The 8 group winners advance.

3

Round 2 — 2 groups of 4, one-strike elimination

The 8 winners are split into 2 groups of 4. Now it's sudden-ish: the lowest score each round gets a strike, and a single strike eliminates you. Last queen standing in each group becomes a finalist.

4

Finals — Best of 3

The two group winners face off head-to-head. First to win 2 out of 3 is crowned Queen of TikTok 👑.
